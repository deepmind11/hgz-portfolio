# Experience: Data Scientist — Standard Chartered Bank

From July 2019 to July 2021, Harshit worked as a Data Scientist at Standard Chartered Bank in Bangalore, India, in the analytics and data science division. Standard Chartered is a multinational banking and financial services corporation.

**Prescriptive ML Model for Sales.** Built a prescriptive machine learning model in R to aid sales teams. The model identified new seven-figure revenue opportunities for the bank.

**Central Data Warehouse.** Enhanced analytical capabilities across five business areas by pooling data from legacy systems into a central data warehouse, unifying data access across multiple lines of business.

**Agile Development.** Followed the Agile Scrum framework for software development, participating in sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives.

This role gave Harshit two years of enterprise data science experience prior to his transition into bioinformatics, including production ML model development, cross-functional stakeholder management, and large-scale data infrastructure work.
