# Education

Harshit holds a Master of Arts (MA) in Biotechnology from Columbia University in New York, completed August 2022 to August 2023, with a 3.80 GPA.

Before Columbia, he earned a Bachelor of Science (BS) in Chemistry from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur from July 2015 to May 2019. He was an INSPIRE Scholarship recipient at IIT Kanpur, awarded by the Indian government for top-ranking science undergraduates.

His undergraduate research at IIT Kanpur included a project on tracking the motion of nanoparticles using Python image analysis, and a summer internship at IIT Bombay studying single-molecule magnets through ab initio quantum chemistry and DFT calculations. That internship led to a co-authored publication in Inorganic Chemistry on record-high magnetic anisotropy in three-coordinate Mn(III) and Cr(II) complexes.
