# Publication

Harshit is a co-author on "Record High Magnetic Anisotropy in Three-Coordinate Mn(III) and Cr(II) Complexes: A Theoretical Perspective," published in Inorganic Chemistry by the American Chemical Society. DOI: 10.1021/acs.inorgchem.1c00978. The work applied ab initio quantum chemistry and DFT calculations to identify magneto-structural correlations that explain record-high magnetic anisotropy in single-molecule magnets.
